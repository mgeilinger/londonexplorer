MAYFAIR



You are stood facing the entrance to an apartment building, its grand doorway framed by ornate pillars and a wrought-iron gate. A marble staircase leads up to the imposing front door, flanked by potted plants and meticulously trimmed hedges.

To the west is Hyde Park, to the north is Marylebone, to the east is Soho and to the south is Westminster.

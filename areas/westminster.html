Westminster
WESTMINSTER



You are on a bustling corner overlooking a large gothic parliamentary building. A sea of smartly dressed office workers moves in waves around you. The air is filled with the sound of traffic, punctuated by the occasional blare of horns and the rumble of buses.

To the west is Belgravia, to the northwest is Mayfair, to the north is Soho, to the northeast is Covent Garden and to the southwest is Chelsea.
